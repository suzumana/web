002 : My favorite sport

　　　My favorite sports are soccer and cheerleading. My favorite team is Yokohama F. Marinos, a team from my hometown Yokohama. I have been going to watch games at Nissan Stadium and Mitsuzawa Stadium since I was a little girl. I love the excitement when a goal is scored or a big save is made, and the sense of unity in the venue. I have also made friends through watching sports, which is another attraction of watching soccer.

　　　Cheerleading is a sport that I have been doing since I was three years old. People tend to think that cheerleading is about cheering for the athletes, but it is also a competitive sport. Cheerleading is a fascinating sport, whether you are doing it yourself or watching it as a spectator. It is a group sport, so a sense of team unity is very important. It is a group sport, so team unity is very important. One of my favorite teams is a Japanese team called "Cheers Factory". They have won the world championship many times. Also, when I actually participated in the American competition, I felt that the level of the American teams was very high, as they are the home of cheerleading. I was impressed by the power and energy of the teams. I hope that cheerleading will become more popular in Japan.
