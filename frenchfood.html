Galette

　　　It is a local dish from the northwestern part of France, and although some people may not like it, it is a crepe made of buckwheat flour. Although it is called a crepe, it is more of a meal than a dessert because it is made of ham, cheese, eggs, and other ingredients wrapped in a crepe. It is eaten in the Brittany region. It is eaten in Brittany, where it is more like a casual meal in a cafe.

Quiche

　　　Quiche, which is familiar to Japanese people, is a staple of French home cooking. It is a local dish from the Lorraine region in northeastern France. Of course, it is best made fresh by hand, but it is also delicious when bought from a delicatessen and reheated. It is a classic warm appetizer that should be eaten simply with a salad as a side dish.Quiche Lorraine, as the basic quiche is called, has very simple ingredients and preparation. A pie crust, mostly called a part-brisée, is lined with bacon, poured with a seasoning of eggs and cream, and baked in the oven.
